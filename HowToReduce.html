Tips to Reduce Waste

1. Get to know the rules of recycling.
Do research and find out the specific rules for your area, this is very important because recycling standards vary from place to place.
2. Ditch plastic bags.
Use cloth bags to pack up your items instead of relying on your supermarket's plastic bags. Leaving them in the trunk of your car will ensure that you won't forget them!
3. Make a meal plan.
In America, food waste is a huge problem. Buying groceries that you know you will eat can help minimize unnecessary waste.
4.Reusable containers.
Storing food properly will extend its life and also keep food fresh for alot longer.
6. Learn to repair rather than discard.
Instead of buying loads of cheap, disposable stuff, buy a few high-quality items and repair them when necessary.
7. Cancel unnecessary mail.
If you cancel unnecessary mail the amount of paper waste you produce will be cut down significantly.
